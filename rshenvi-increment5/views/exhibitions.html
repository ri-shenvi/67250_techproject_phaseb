In the spirit of the museum: scale, perspective, and wonder

MonoMuse shares the same impulse as science and design museums: help visitors see the world differently. The short film Powers of Ten (1977) by the Eames Office is a classic piece of educational media—often used in museum and classroom settings—that zooms from human scale out to the cosmos and inward to the atomic scale.
