Membership & donations

Whether you are visiting for the first time, studying in Pittsburgh, or already part of our community, you can help MonoMuse grow. Memberships keep our doors open for students and neighbors; donations fund new exhibitions, artist residencies, and free public programs.

Who you are

  • Guest / visitor: Explore the museum with a day ticket, then decide if you want to come back often—members save on every visit.
  • Student: Discounted student membership includes unlimited admission and invites to student nights and workshops.
  • Member: Full members receive guest passes, shop discounts, and early access to ticketed events.

Donations

Tax-deductible gifts of any size support conservation, education, and free days for the public. One-time and recurring donations are welcome. Common gift levels are $25, $50, $100, and $250—you choose an amount that fits your budget.

  • Education fund: School visits, teen labs, and digital learning kits.
  • Exhibition fund: New commissions and traveling shows at MonoMuse.
  • Where it is needed most: Flexible support directed by museum leadership.

This site is a course project. To “join” or give in a real deployment, you would connect these options to a payment or CRM system. For now, use tickets for a visit and treat this page as the information your visitors need to support the museum.
